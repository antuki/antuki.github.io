A first article to celebrate the creation of this blog thanks to Hugo and blogdown. I can now create posts (in French and / or English) in one click from R.

It is also an opportunity to share with you my discovery of the emojifont package which allows to add small images to our ggplots. Thank you @guangchuangyu!

#install.packages("emojifont")
library(emojifont)

library(ggplot2)
emoji('blue_heart')
## [1] "<f0><U+009F><U+0092><U+0099>"
t= seq(-1, 1, length=50)
x=cos(t)*sin(t)*log(abs(t))
y=sqrt(abs(t))*cos(t)
#windows()
ggplot() + geom_emoji('blue_heart', x=x, y=y, size=10, color="#00BCF2")
